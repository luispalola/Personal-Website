About Me

Hi! My name is Luis Palola and I am currently a senior at Rutgers University, majoring in Computer Science. I plan to work as a software engineer or in some other tech-related job in the industry right after college. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by anything tech-related. Whether it’s video games or mobile devices, I’ve always wanted to know how and why they work. I am passionate about technology and I want to help make a lasting impact in how technology shapes our world now and in the future.
